Zapier Workflow Demo



This automation improves sales team efficiency by linking lead capture, follow-ups, and internal communications into a seamless, fully automated workflow. Each step triggers the next, reducing manual work, preventing errors, and ensuring timely action on every lead.

Step-by-Step Workflow

  1. Customer receives an email inviting them to fill out a Google Form.
  2. Upon submission, the Google Form updates a live Google Sheet with the new lead.
  3. Zapier detects the new row in the Google Sheet and triggers an automated follow-up email through Calendly.
  4. After the email is sent, Zapier posts the lead information to the appropriate Slack channel based on department.
  5. Slack generates a ticket number for supervisors to assign follow-up tasks to team members.
  6. The Google Sheet is updated automatically to log the completed actions for tracking and reporting.

  • Usefulness: Streamlines lead management, ensures real-time database updates, improves team coordination, and enhances process efficiency.
