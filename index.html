About Me

I’m a data analyst with a focus on marketing operations and business analytics, currently pursuing my Master’s in Business Analytics at Hult International Business School. I specialize in turning complex data into actionable insights that improve decision-making, optimize campaigns, and streamline operations through process improvements. My toolkit includes Python, SQL, Tableau, and Power BI, along with hands-on experience in data cleaning, pipeline automation, predictive modeling, and dashboard development. I’m especially skilled at using data to evaluate marketing performance, track KPIs, and design reporting systems that give stakeholders clarity and confidence.

My portfolio highlights projects in marketing performance analysis, operations optimization, and automation, as well as exploratory work in machine learning and dynamic content generation. I’ve collaborated with cross-functional teams to build models, deliver interactive dashboards, and present insights that connect technical detail with business value. I believe the best analytics work combines clarity, context, and impact—not just answering questions, but surfacing better ones. Whether building a campaign performance dashboard or designing a data-driven automation tool, I focus on driving measurable outcomes and supporting smarter, faster decisions.
